Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a hearty Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese.

Perfect for family dinners or gatherings, it's rich, cheesy, and full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 700g tomato sauce
  • 2 cups ricotta or béchamel sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
  • Salt, pepper, oregano

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Boil noodles until soft, then drain.
  3. In a pan, cook beef with onion and garlic until browned. Add tomato sauce and spices. Simmer 10 mins.
  4. In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella. Repeat layers.
  5. Top with Parmesan and bake for 30–40 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  6. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
