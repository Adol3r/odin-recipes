Spaghetti Bolognese

Home

Description

A classic Italian pasta dish made with a rich, meaty tomato sauce. Perfect for family dinners and easy to prepare.

This recipe combines ground beef, onions, garlic, tomatoes, and herbs to make a hearty meal.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 300g ground beef
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper
  • Italian herbs

Steps

  1. Boil spaghetti until al dente.
  2. Heat oil, saut√© onions and garlic.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Add tomatoes, herbs, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Serve sauce over spaghetti.
