Chicken Curry

Home

Description

A flavorful and spicy Indian chicken curry that's perfect with rice or naan.

Made with tender chicken pieces simmered in a rich curry sauce.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast, cubed
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp ginger
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil, cook onions, garlic, and ginger.
  2. Add chicken and brown it.
  3. Sprinkle curry powder and stir well.
  4. Add coconut milk and simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with rice.
